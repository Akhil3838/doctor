Blockchain for Good:

How Blockchain is Solving the Sustainable Development Goals
✍ Dr. Vin Menon 📅 May 1, 2023

Blockchain For Good is a groundbreaking book that explores how blockchain technology is solving the UN's Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) and creating a more sustainable and equitable future.

Dr. Vin Menon provides a comprehensive overview of blockchain technology and its applications in food, health, shelter, education, economic growth, environmental challenges, and social issues. This book is a must-read for anyone interested in the intersection of technology, sustainability, and social impact.

Through the book, readers will learn about the 5 key benefits of blockchain technology, how it can empower the unbanked and solve the world's hunger crisis, how it can revolutionize the healthcare and education industries, and how it can combat climate change and promote sustainable industrialization.

In This Book, You Will:

  • Understand the basics of blockchain technology and its benefits.
  • Explore how blockchain technology is solving the UN's Sustainable Development Goals.
  • Discover real-world examples of blockchain technology in action in various industries.
  • Learn about the potential of blockchain technology to promote sustainability, equity, and social impact.
Join the revolution of using blockchain for good and make a difference in the world.
