Dr. Vin Menon

With more than two decades of experience in technology innovation, sustainable finance and environmental leadership, I have dedicated my career to bridging the worlds of digital transformation and ecological sustainability. My mission has always been clear: to align technological progress with the restoration of our planet's natural systems.

As Co-founder and CEO of AQUAE, I lead the development of the AQUAE Labs Ecosystem Conservation Index (ALCI), an innovative, patent-pending framework that redefines how ecosystems are valued. By transforming oxygen, biodiversity, biomass and primary ecosystem services into measurable, insured and tradable assets, we are building a new financial language for nature. This pioneering work positions AQUAE at the forefront of sustainable finance, bridging ecological restoration with global capital markets and advancing the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).
